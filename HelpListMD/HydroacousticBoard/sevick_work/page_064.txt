Another limitation is encountered in the high-impedance use of
these transformers, especially where high efficiency is important. As
will be shown in Chapter 11, on materials and power ratings, most
ferrites exhibit greater loss at higher impedance levels. These losses
appear to be dielectric losses, and not magnetic losses as with conven-
tional transformers.
