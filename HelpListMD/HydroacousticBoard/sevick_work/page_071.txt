Even though the Guanella ununs are more complicated than that
of the single transmission line approach of Ruthroff’s, measurements
made on these transformers, using the simple test equipment in Chapter
12, show much less phase shift at the higher frequencies, and hence
greater high-frequency response. They also lend themselves more
readily to hybrid use.
